BizIQ ESG Visibility is an ESG compliance, monitoring, and intelligence platform built for the maritime and supply-chain ecosystem. It helps companies measure, track, and improve ESG performance while making their sustainability efforts visible and comparable to banks, investors, charterers, cargo owners, and regulators.

The platform is designed for:

  • Ship owners and ship managers
  • Charterers and cargo owners
  • Ports and terminal operators
  • Multimodal transport operators (MTOs)
  • Logistics and supply-chain companies
  • Marine equipment and OEM manufacturers

It is also used by financial institutions, investors, and infrastructure funds to assess ESG performance and risk.

No. BizIQ ESG Visibility Platform is not a certification body and does not replace statutory reporting. It works alongside existing ESG, BRSR, CSRD, sustainability, and operational reports by converting them into decision-ready ESG intelligence aligned with global standards.

BizIQ maps ESG data across major global and national frameworks, including:

  • GRI
  • TCFD
  • CSRD / ESRS
  • BRSR (India)
  • EU Taxonomy
  • IMO, CII, EEXI
  • Poseidon Principles
  • SDGs
  • Maritime India Vision & Harit Sagar

This allows clients to view ESG performance through multiple stakeholder lenses in one platform.

The platform uses a combination of:

  • Client-uploaded ESG, annual, CSR, BRSR, and sustainability reports
  • Operational and asset-level data (vessels, ports, logistics assets)
  • Publicly available regulatory and industry data

An AI-powered engine extracts, structures, and maps ESG information as per global frameworks automatically.
